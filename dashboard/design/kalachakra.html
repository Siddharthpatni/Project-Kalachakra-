The Proper Sum

How fifty domains become one number.

Probabilities don't add — log-odds do. Each layer contributes a signed, gated, weighted nudge; these sum onto a baseline prior and pass through calibration. A negative layer (uncertainty, rigor) is allowed to subtract confidence.
