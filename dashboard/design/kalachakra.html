The Proper Sum

How fifty domains become one number.

Probabilities do not add — log-odds do. Each layer contributes a signed nudge to the log-odds, which sum onto a baseline prior and pass through calibration. Positive (cyan) pushes the event more likely; negative (vermilion) is a domain that reduces confidence — uncertainty and rigor are allowed to subtract.

The Stages

Fifty domains, one falsifiable question.

Each domain is a stage in the pipeline, not decoration. Foundations are built and validated first; higher domains earn their weight only if they add out-of-sample skill. Lit tiles are shipped and tested.

Scientific disclaimer. This is a research instrument, not an oracle. It does not provide deterministic predictions or claims about individual fate. It estimates whether specific mathematical encodings of astronomical configurations carry statistically detectable predictive information, and reports every forecast with calibrated uncertainty and a comparison against strong baselines. H₀ — that these features add nothing — stands until the data rejects it.
